Dessert Table

Apart from wedding dresses, food and drinks, dessert are also an important part of any wedding ceremony. Guests are served with a variety of desserts including candy, fruit and a variety of interesting and tasty desserts. There’s nothing sweeter than saying your vows before all of your family and friends—except for maybe the dessert table. Eating cake is undoubtedly one of the highlights of your wedding, and having a beautifully decorated dessert table will entice your guests to sample all of the goodies.

There are different types of desserts such as candy, fruits, cupcakes, cookies and others
